Island Birding Tips 🏝️
  • Prime action: 6:30–9:00 AM & 4:30–6:00 PM before & after the heat hits.
  • Flowers & hedges = hummingbirds & bananaquits. Water & rocks = pelicans & egrets.
  • Look up late morning for frigatebirds riding the thermals like kites.
  • Find a shady spot, sit still 3–5 minutes, let the birds come to you. Bonus: works great with a drink in hand.
